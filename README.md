# zenher-ai
Zenher AI is an interactive, AI-powered chatbot designed to provide personalized health insights, with a specific focus on menstrual health and wellness. Offering a user-friendly and intuitive interface, Zenher AI delivers concise, accurate, and relevant answers to users' queries, acting as an interactive health assistant.

Features
AI Integration: Provides personalized, AI-generated health insights based on user queries.

Focus on Menstrual Health: Specializes in menstrual health and wellness, offering tailored advice and information.

User-Friendly Design: Simple, intuitive interface that ensures seamless interaction for users.

Concise Responses: Delivers quick, relevant, and clear answers to health-related queries.

Interactive Health Assistant: Acts as a virtual assistant for users seeking wellness advice or guidance.
